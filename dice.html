What do you need to start playing ttrpg

Here we will give you some advice to get in the amazing world of the tabletop Rpg. In many times people think in dungeons and drangons whe we talk about role playing games but in the reality we have many kinds of games for your group imagine that is like a chips flavor.

Find what you like
What kind of game you like is essential, you want to have fun in your tabletop so this is your first step, wanna play a good old medieval game, or maybe something with more neon like a ciberpunk game, is up to you what kind of adventure do want to play
Find your playgroup
Finding people to play is also important Rpg is about colaborative storytelling and for this you will need a group to play. Find a play group is an simple task, you can look for groups in foruns, discord servers and friends.
Get your gear
To play RPG you will need some gear for this you can play in virtual tabletop like, roll20, Foundry and many other or you can play in person with your group. For both you will need your character sheet and the rulebook for learn the rules and understand what your character can do.
